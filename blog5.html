Sustainable Travel in Bhutan

By David Wilson June 1, 2023

Bhutan, a small Himalayan kingdom nestled between India and China, has long been celebrated for its commitment to environmental preservation and sustainable development. As a responsible traveler, exploring this unique destination can be an opportunity to experience the beauty of nature while minimizing your carbon footprint.

"Sustainable travel is not just a trend; it's a way of life that preserves the beauty of our world for future generations."

- Unknown

One of the key pillars of sustainable tourism in Bhutan is the country's "high-value, low-impact" tourism policy. By implementing a daily sustainable development fee for international visitors, the government aims to limit the number of tourists while ensuring that the revenue generated contributes to the conservation of natural resources and the preservation of cultural heritage.
