About Drukyul Fly and High Bhutan

Discover the true essence of Bhutan with our team of passionate travel experts.

Our Story

Drukyul Fly and High Bhutan was founded with the vision of sharing the breathtaking beauty and rich cultural heritage of Bhutan with the world. Our team of experienced travel professionals has a deep love and appreciation for this enchanting country, and we strive to create unforgettable experiences for our clients.

To enable our valued customers to feel the essence of being a tourist in Bhutan

Empowering Cheaper options for travel

To satisty our customers with their every visits
