Embark on an Unforgettable Adventure in Bhutan

Explore the rugged landscapes, trek through scenic mountain trails, and immerse yourself in the breathtaking natural beauty of this Himalayan paradise.

Adventure Tour Highlights

White Water Rafting in Bhutan

Experience the adrenaline rush of white water rafting through Bhutan's pristine rivers, surrounded by stunning natural landscapes and towering mountains. Paddle through rapids, navigate thrilling turns, and create unforgettable memories.

Tour Itinerary:

  • Raft through the exhilarating rapids of the Mo Chu and Pho Chu rivers.
  • Enjoy scenic camping under the stars along the riverbanks.
  • Explore traditional villages and witness the unique culture of remote communities.
  • Hike through scenic trails and visit ancient monasteries tucked away in the mountains.

Mountain Biking Adventures

Explore the rugged terrain and breathtaking landscapes of Bhutan on an exhilarating mountain biking adventure. Ride through remote villages, descend challenging trails, and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of this Himalayan kingdom.

Tour Itinerary:

  • Ride through the scenic Phobjikha Valley, home to the rare black-necked cranes.
  • Challenge yourself on the technical trails of the Punakha region.
  • Descend through lush forests and cross mountain streams on your bike.
  • Interact with local communities and learn about their way of life.
