Discover Bhutan's Most Popular Destinations

Explore the iconic sights and hidden gems of this enchanting Himalayan kingdom.

Top Destinations

Thimphu

The capital city of Bhutan, Thimphu offers a unique blend of traditional architecture and modern amenities. Visit the iconic Buddha Dordenma statue, explore ancient dzongs (fortresses), and immerse yourself in the vibrant local markets.

Book Now

Paro

Nestled in a picturesque valley, Paro is home to the iconic Tiger's Nest Monastery, perched precariously on a cliffside. Explore ancient temples, hike through scenic trails, and witness the rich cultural heritage of this beautiful region.

Book Now

Punakha

Punakha is renowned for its stunning dzongs, including the magnificent Punakha Dzong, which stands at the confluence of two rivers. Discover the fertile valleys, visit traditional villages, and immerse yourself in the serene natural beauty of this region.
